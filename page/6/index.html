Rubrics for teacher observations are garbage

There have been a lot of bad ideas foisted onto educators over the course of my career. One of the ones that always pissed me off was the use of a rubric for teacher observation. Specifically, using the Danielson Framework. The Danielson Framework is a LONG laundry list of topics and concepts and for each a teacher could be rated ineffective, developing, effective, or highly effective. It's garbage. Sure, there are some good things in the framework but using a restrictive rubric to judge a teacher is just a bad idea.
